Yosemite

Located in California’s Sierra Nevada mountains, Yosemite is famed for its giant, ancient sequoia trees, and for Tunnel View, the iconic vista of towering Bridalveil Fall and the granite cliffs of El Capitan and Half Dome.

Joshua Tree National Park

Named for the region’s twisted, bristled Joshua trees, the park straddles the Colorado Desert and the Mojave Desert and is characterized by rugged rock formations and stark desert landscapes.
